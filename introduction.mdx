## What is Mais Hábito?

Mais Hábito ("More Habit" in Portuguese) is a **single-player productivity app** focused on building consistent routines through gamification. The app transforms habit-building into an engaging progression system where completing tasks and challenges earns XP, builds streaks, and levels up your profile.

The application is a React-based frontend that connects to the [Mais Hábito API](https://github.com/RafaeltiMoreira/mais-habito-api) for data persistence and business logic.

| Routing | React Router DOM 7 |

## How It Works

The app follows a simple progression loop:

1. **Sign up** and create your account
2. **Browse challenges** from the catalog or create your own templates
3. **Accept a challenge** to start a timed consistency track
4. **Complete daily tasks** linked to your challenges to earn XP
5. **Write notes** in your progress journal to reflect on your journey
6. **Track your streak** on the dashboard to stay motivated

<Note>
  Mais Hábito is a frontend-only project. It requires a running instance of the [Mais Hábito API](https://github.com/RafaeltiMoreira/mais-habito-api) to function. Configure the API URL in your `.env` file before starting.
</Note>
